The Best Movies According to MOHIT RATHORE

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Dangal

Dangal movie is real story of Mahavir singh phogat who was a wrestler

Bahubali

In ancient India, an adventurous and daring man becomes involved in a decades-old feud between two warring peoples. It's a tale of two cousins in the Kingdom of Mahishmati, India. Balla fights his way with cousin Baahubali for the throne.

3 idiots

Narrated through two parallel dramas, one in the present and the other set ten years in the past, the story follows the friendship of three students at an Indian engineering college and is a satire about the social pressures under the Indian education system.
